Statutory Audit
(Hong Kong)

According to the Companies Ordinance, the financial statements of a limited company incorporated in Hong Kong is required to be audited by a qualified auditor annually.

For taxation purpose, companies incorporated in Hong Kong must submit an audited financial statement to the Inland Revenue Department (IRD) together with their Profits Tax Return (PTR).
